TEAM BLUE BIO

Project and Sponsor

The Ministry of Citizens’ Services provides a wide range of services to British Columbians across the province. Our project is to create a Digital Product Playbook that guides ministry workers through the process of ideation, development, and ongoing support of a digital product. In addition, this playbook will be validated by our own process of creating a simple application within the BC Government framework
